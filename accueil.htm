Streetlifting Calculator

What is Streetlifting?

Streetlifting is a version of weighted calisthenics and a competitive strength discipline that combines
elements of calisthenics and powerlifting that focuses on 4 mouvements using a 1 rep max format

  • weighted pull ups

  • weighted Dips

  • weighted Muscles-ups

  • beltless Barbell Squats

The one rep max format

  • Each competitor has 3 attempts to lift the heaviest weight possible for each mouvement
  • You can retry the previous weight if you failed it
  • The weights are not added together , only your heaviest is counted

How to calculate your 1RM

One of the most popular ways is using the Epley formula. in Streetlifting the formula is a modified
by adding your bodyweight to the weight lifted

1RM = ((bodyweight + weight) x (1 + 0.0333 x reps)) - bodyweight
